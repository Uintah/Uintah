for Reta -- Rwvn cases, there are with Ib (Planck function) CDF, and
without Ib CDF files to get wavenumber from a chosen R random number.


RwvnabcsNosorting2.dat -- the long version of R-CDF for T=1000K
cold section WITH Ib CDF. includs a bunch of zeros k's wavenumbers

RwvnabcsNosorting2.dat -- the shorten version of R-CDF for T=1000K
cold section WITH Ib CDF, from RwvnabcsNosorting.dat

RwvnabcsNoIb.dat -- R-CDF without Ib for T=1000K.

RwvnabcsNosorting1500K-CO***** -- the shorten version R-CDF WITH Ib
for T = 1500 K hot section.

RwvnabcsNoIb1500K-CO202H2O04CO006.dat -- the WITHOUT Ib R-CDF for T =
1500 K hot section.


FSKabscnoIb.cc -- to generate R-CDF without using Ib. R = \frac{int
k}{int_all k}


FSKabscwvnRDir.cc -- generate R-CDF with Ib, 
R = \frac{int_0^eta Ibeta keta}{int_all Ibeta keta}
