ScaledFrame Widget Help

The ScaledFrame Widget consists of the Frame Widget with a short cylindrical "slider" on two of the four long cylindrical sides. These two sides are orthogonal. The primary use of the ScaledFrame Widget is to set the u and v densities of streamlines emerging from its edges or from throughout its surface.

The new "slider" cylinders may be dragged along the cylindrical sides, but not off of them or past their ends. The distances along the cylindrical sides is then used to get the densities. The rest is as on the Frame Widget.
