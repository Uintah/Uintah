ScaledBox Widget Help

The ScaledBox Widget consists of the Box Widget with a short cylindrical "slider" on three of the twelve long cylindrical sides. These three sides are orthogonal. The primary use of the Scaled Box Widget is to set the u, v, and w densities of vector arrows within its interior. When the widget is used in this manner it is referred to as a "hedgehog."

The new "slider" cylinders may be dragged along the cylindrical sides, but not off of them or past their ends. The distances along the cylindrical sides is then used to get the densities. The rest is as on the Box Widget.
